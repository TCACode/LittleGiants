About Dr. Reddick


Niles Reddick is the son of Harold and Beverly Reddick and born in Savannah, Georgia, He went to college at Valdosta State University and Florida State University.He currently lives with his wife, Michelle and his 2 children Audrey and Nicholas in Jackson, Tennessee. Dr. Reddick has written 4 books, Drifting Too Far From the Shore,Finding My Way Home, Reading The Coffee Grounds and Roadkill Art and Other Oddities.
Dr. Reddick is currently the Vice Provost at the University of Memphis Lambuth Campus in Jackson, Tennessee.
